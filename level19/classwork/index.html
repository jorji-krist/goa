small information abt clothes

1. Casual Wear
T-Shirts: Simple, comfortable, and versatile, often made of cotton.
Jeans: Sturdy pants made from denim, popular worldwide.
Sweatshirts/Hoodies: Cozy, casual tops, great for cooler weather.
b Shorts: Casual lower wear, ideal for warm weather.
Casual Dresses: Loose-fitting dresses for a relaxed look.
2. Formal Wear
Suits: Matched jacket and trousers or skirt, usually worn with a dress shirt and tie.
Dresses: Elegant dresses often worn for formal events like weddings or work functions.
Blazers: Semi-formal jackets that can be worn with slacks or jeans.
Dress Pants and Skirts: More tailored than casual pants, suitable for business or events.
3. Athletic Wear (Activewear)
Leggings: Stretchy pants often worn for yoga, gym, or running.
Tracksuits: Matching top and bottom, comfortable for exercise or casual outings.
Sports Bras and Tank Tops: Supportive tops for workouts.
Shorts and Tights: Lightweight, often moisture-wicking fabrics for comfort during physical activity.
